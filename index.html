Ivan Gudoshnikov

Complex Materials Lab @ Arizona State University

ivangudoshnikov [at] gmail.com

Hi! Welcome to my page! I currently do research in Mathematics and Mechanics with Prof. Oleg Makarenkov from the University of Texas at Dallas and Prof. Yang Jiao from Arizona State University. I have recently (May 2020) graduated from UT Dallas with a PhD in Mathematics, worked as a postdoc at ASU, and currently is transitioning to a new position in Europe, which is about to start in March 2021.

Interests
